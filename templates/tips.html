Jewelry Tips for Your Face Shape

Here’s some expert advice on which jewelry suits different face shapes. Make the right choices to enhance your beauty!

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! Almost any jewelry style will suit you. For earrings, go for hoops or drop earrings to accentuate your natural symmetry. For necklaces, try pendants or choker-style necklaces to highlight your neckline.

Round Face Shape

For round faces, the goal is to add length and angles. Long, dangling earrings and sharp geometric designs will give your face a more elongated appearance. A V-shaped necklace or a long pendant necklace is a great choice to create the illusion of a longer neck.

Square Face Shape

Square faces benefit from jewelry that softens sharp angles. Opt for round, curvy earrings like hoops or studs. A soft, flowing necklace will also add contrast to your defined jawline, balancing out the strong angles of your face.

Oblong Face Shape

For oblong faces, the aim is to add width. Go for earrings that are wider than they are long, like studs or button earrings. For necklaces, shorter styles like chokers or collars will create the illusion of more width and complement the length of your face.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and narrow chin. To balance your features, try long earrings like drops or teardrops. A necklace with a sweetheart or oval shape can help soften the angles of your chin and add harmony to your face shape.
